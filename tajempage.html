JFC

Tajem is the abbreviation of Tanggul-Jember. The Tajem requirement is used for activities to enliven the Republic of Indonesia's independence day in which people walk 30 km from the Tanggul to Jember.

Tajem activities are carried out in August every year. More than 10 thousand people were registered in each implementation. The editorial has the aim of tracing the fighters of Indonesian independence during colonial times who carried out guerrillas by walking tens of kilometers.
