JFC

Jember Fashion Carnaval (Indonesia: Jember Clothing Carnival) or often called JFC is a fashion carnival event which is held annually in Jember Regency, East Java. This carnival was initiated by Dynand Fariz who is also the founder of the JFC Center. [1] As many as 2000 participants took part in the 4 days of the event including Kids Carnival, Artwear Carnival, Waci, and Grand Carnival. On the main street of the city of Jember witnessed by hundreds of thousands of spectators on the right and left of the road. They are divided into 10 defiles, each of which reflects the fashion trends of the year.
