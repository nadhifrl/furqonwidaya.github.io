JEMBER FASHION CARNAVAL

JFC is a carnival outfit that is held every year in Jember Regency, East Java. This carnival was initiated by Dynand Fariz who is also the founder of the JFC Center.

As many as 2000 participants took part in the 4-day event that was held for the Children's Carnival, Artwear Carnival, Waci and Grand Carnival. On the main street of the city of Jember witnessed by thousands of visitors on the right and left of the road. They are divided into 10 defiles, each of which reflects the trend of the year claimed.

The first unclean is the Najis with the theme of national clothing from certain regions such as Java, Bali, Madura, Dayak, Papua, Sumatra, and so on. Another defile is the theme of a fashion trend whether from a country, certain group, film, event or other global event. All clothes are made in the form of costumes, all of which are competed for awards.

In programming JFC has the concept of 4E (Education, Entertainment, Exhibition, Economic Benefit):

  1. Education (Education). Through in-house training the participants are given the knowledge of fashion design, fashion run way, fashion dance, presenters, make-up and makeup and through the competition it is hoped that human resources will be born confident, born instructors, leaders, choreographers, presenters, singers, entrepreneurs and others -other. Through exploring the potential of participants by providing opportunities for the development of creativity through competition, new ideas will be born in the field of dance, designing clothing, accessories, and others. For the audience also added insight into regional cultures and diverse outside cultures.
  2. Entertainment (Entertainment). As an exclusive event that can be entertainment for the whole community from all walks of life, profession, age, education, economic background and so on. Exhibition. Being a center of learning and research about fashion carnivals, being the object of taking photos for professional photographers and many others.
  3. Exhibition. Being a center of learning and research about fashion carnivals, being the object of taking photos for professional photographers and many others.
  4. Economic Benefits (economic development). Through the implementation of events that have a clear concept, quality human resources, sustainable, interesting, obtaining support from the community, the government and people's representatives allow it to become a leading tourism potential that can move other tourism potential (hospitality, restaurants, transportation, souvenirs, special food, and others).
