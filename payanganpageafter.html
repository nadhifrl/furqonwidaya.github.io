PAYANGAN BEACH

Payangan Beach is one of the unique tourist attractions in Jember Regency. Not only beach tours are offered, but, there are some things that can be found when visiting Payangan Beach, Jember Regency, East Java. The uniqueness of this beach is the panorama of three beaches with three hills and one bay.

The three beaches are located between three prominent hills. These hills can be climbed because they have footpaths that are used by visitors. From the top of this hill, views of Payangan Ambulu Jember Beach can be seen almost in its entirety. You will see the beauty of this beach from the hill.
