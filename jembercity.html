Jember Regency is a regency of East Java province, Indonesia. Which has thousands of cities in Jember. Jember is famous for its tobacco farms and traditional food called tape which is made of fermented cassava.


This Regency bordering Probolinggo Regency and Bondowowso Regency in the north, Banyuwangi Regency in the east, to south lies the Indian Ocean in the south, and Lumajang Regency in the west. Jember Regency located in the Tapal Kuda region, East Java. Jember Regency consists of 31 districts
