PAPUMA BEACH

Papuma Beach is a white sandy beach with beautiful turquoise blue water. There are coral stones that adorn Papuma Beach to present an increasingly attractive charm. The existence of Papuma Beach itself has never been separated from a number of myths.

Even the myths that exist on this beach are probably the most among the beaches in Indonesia. There are at least four myths that are owned by Papuma Beach. One of them is the existence of a cave that is believed by the community as a place of meditation of the Kyai Mataram and a place to live Dewi Sri Wulan, one of the southern queen's daughters
