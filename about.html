ABOUT

 

Jember Tourism Guide is a tourism website that presents the beauty of tourism and event in the Regency of Jember.


This Regency bordering Probolinggo Regency and Bondowowso Regency in the north, Banyuwangi Regency in the east, to south lies the Indian Ocean in the south, and Lumajang Regency in the west. Jember Regency located in the Tapal Kuda region, East Java. The goal is to help promote tourism in the city of Jember throughout the world.
