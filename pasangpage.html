PASANG MOUNTAIN

Although the distance and road access are challenging, but the charm offered will make anyone not be disappointed. Especially for fans of traveling and instagramable photo spot hunters, this place deserves to be a must-visit tourist destination. Especially when waiting for the sun to set, really a phenomenon that should not be wasted.

Not just enjoying the nature, you can also try exciting facilities provided by the managers.
